'The Real Housewives of New York' Season 8 Taglines Revealed

Publish date: 2024-08-19

They’re again ready to take Manhattan — and everyone else should take cover! The Real Housewives of New York City‘s eighth season premieres on Wednesday, April 6, and Us Weekly can exclusively reveal the video of the seven fabulous ladies’ taglines.

This season, the Bravo series sees socialite and mom of two Julianne “Jules” Wainstein joining the fray. The returning cast members are Bethenny Frankel, Carole Radziwill, Dorinda Medley, Luann de Lesseps, Ramona Singer and Sonja Morgan. (In the previous season, viewers saw the last of Heather Thomson and Kristen Taekman, who left the show.)

“If you can’t handle the truth, you can’t handle me,” says Frankel in her tagline. The Skinnygirl boss rejoined the series in season 7 after departing following season 3.

Morgan boasts, “If being Sonja is so wrong, why does it feel so right?”

Viewers this season will see Frankel returning to the dating scene amid new health issues, Radziwill struggling to define her relationship with younger man Adam, and Morgan adjusting to her newly empty nest.
