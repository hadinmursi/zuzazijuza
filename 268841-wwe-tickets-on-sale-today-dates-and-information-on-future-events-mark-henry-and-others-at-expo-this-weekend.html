WWE Tickets On Sale Today: Dates and Information on Future Events, Mark Henry and Others At Expo Thi

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Tickets go on sale to the general public today for a few of the following WWE events:

– WWE Raw taping at the Nationwide Arena in Columbus, OH on Monday, April 29th.

– WWE Raw live event at the Wesbanco Arena in Wheeling, WV on Saturday, May 4.

-WWE Smackdown taping at the PNC Arena in Raleigh, NC on Tuesday, May 7th.

– David Otunga, Mark Henry, Aksana and Kaitlyn are appearing at the Muscle & Fitness booth at this weekend's Arnold Fitness Expo in Columbus, OH. They will be at the booth this afternoon from 3pm-5pm signing autographs and taking pictures. Many of the pictures have already been posted on Twitter.
