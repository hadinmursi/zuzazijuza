Publish date: 2024-08-27
Lily-Rose Melody Depp (born May 27, 1999), born to Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis’ parents, is a model and French-American actress. Depp began her acting career with a minor part in Tusk (2014) and started to star in the period dramas The Dancer (2016), in which she played Isadora Duncan, Planetarium (2016), and The King (2019). Depp has been nominated for two César Awards for Most Promising Actress for her roles in The Dancer and A Faithful Man (2018), respectively. For her role in My Last Lullaby, she won the Best Actress Award at the fourth Los Angeles Film Awards (2019).
