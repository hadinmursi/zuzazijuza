Wiki Biography

Winnie Johnson-Marquart is President of the Johnson Family Foundation. She is tied with her 3 siblings and mother at #215 on the Forbes 400 list of Richest Americans.She is the daughter of Samuel Curtis Johnson, Jr., who died in 2004, and Imogene Powers Johnson and the great great granddaughter of S. C. Johnson & Son founder Samuel Curtis Johnson, Sr. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
