Who Is Tessa Brooks? 8 Interesting Facts About The Thriving Influencer

Publish date: 2024-09-01

If you’re a dance enthusiast, chances are you know who Tessa Brooks is. She might not be as popular as Addison Rae or the D’Amelios, but she is one of the most popular dancers on the internet, in addition to being a fashion and beauty creator. Brooks was born and raised in Fresno, California, but later moved to Los Angeles to follow her dreams.

Her YouTube channel, which is now dedicated to her vlogs and makeup tutorials, has over 3.3 million followers. Her dance videos which she became known for, she now posts on her TikTok account.

Tessa Brooks turns 24 next month, but the influencer has achieved so much already. She started out as a dancer but ventured into vlogging and acting. We’ve curated a list of interesting facts about Tessa Brooks, including her healthy habits and how the busy creator spends her downtime.

8 Tessa Brooks’ Impact On Social Media

Tessa Brooks is one of the most popular digital stars of her generation, with a combined following of 29 million. A huge part of Tessa's following is due to her authentic content, which spans beauty, lifestyle, fashion, and vlogs. Though, it is because of her humble personality, relentless work ethic, and candor that she’s earned so many loyal followers and that keeps coming back.

Although she has one of the largest followings in the game, Tessa has stayed real about mental health and social media's effects which is comparable to how fellow influencer Alix Earle uses her platform to empower others. Her platform has been dedicated to helping others and discussing the misconceptions and struggles of the digital age.

7 Tessa Brooks Practices These Healthy Habits

Tessa Brooks is keen on staying physically and mentally healthy. Years ago, Tessa went through a dark time when work took over, and she neglected self-care rituals and routines, which gave her the passion to speak on the topic of mental health. Many other public figures have been vocal about mental health in regard to weight loss/gain, including Doja Cat, who is not afraid to clap back at trolls who love body shame celebrities when they lose or gain weight.

In addition to gaining weight, Tessa Brooks was in a bad mental state. Tessa learned a valuable lesson though: she truly believes mental and physical health go hand in hand, and so she fully committed to getting back to a good place with a strict routine and emphasis on balance, practice, and consistency. It took her two years, but she committed to her morning workouts, cryotherapy, consistently taking her vitamins, and drinking the right amount of water for her body weight. Brooks also maintains a healthy sleep schedule along with a few hours of fasting before sleep, and the creator also doesn’t consume alcohol. Using these techniques, Tessa Brooks was able to feel like herself again, and she wants to share and help others who are struggling.

6 Tessa Brooks Is Taking Her Fashion Aesthetic To The Next Level

Tessa Brooks has recently honed in on her fashion sense and is taking her style aesthetic to the next level. Brooks has worked with brands like Tommy Hilfiger, Etro, Tory Burch, AKNVAS and others. Her influence in the fashion space is quickly growing; Tessa just sat front row at the Tory Burch New York Fashion Week show, and attended Vogue, Saks and Daily Front Row’s Fashion Media Awards which also took place during the New York Fashion Week.

5 Tessa Brooks Has Also Partnered With These Brands

Tessa Brooks has partnered with many of the most recognizable brands around the world as she continues to build her audience and break into new markets. She dropped her first capsule collection in 2021, a collaboration with the clothing brand Boohoo. She also partnered with LSPACE in 2022 to launch a multi-capsule collection

Brooks recently took part in a Marc Jacobs makeup campaign for Paris Fashion Week, in addition to partnering with Maybelline, Smashbox, Clinique, and ASICS and several other big brands.

4 Tessa Brooks Has Been In Showbiz From A Young Age

Tessa Brooks is a multi-talented creator who has been a dancer since she was 12 years old. She has choreographed and performed on The Voice, Kids Choice Awards, The Teen Choice Awards and Radio Disney. Dance has always been a passion for her, but at 14 years old, she saw it as a means of connecting to her loved ones.

She began posting dance videos online after moving to LA for her dance career; her videos then led to her making connections with other creators, and so her social media influence started to grow. As a result of her commitment to her craft her following grew pretty quickly.

3 Tessa Brooks Is Also An Actress, Here’s What She’s Appeared In

Tessa Brooks is not just a dancer or social media creator. The 23-year-old star has also been in a few film/TV shows. She first appeared in Jake Paul’s music video for ‘Its Everyday Bro’ featuring the musical/dance ensemble Team 10 (created by Jake Paul) which Tessa Brooks was also a part of. Tessa Brooks also starred in Boss Cheer (2018-2019), a series by Brat TV which chronicles the lives and struggles of the members of a competitive cheerleading team.

Brooks would then appear as MzTiff in an episode of Happy Hazel in 2020. Her latest role however would be to play Jenny in the 2021 movie Honey Girls alongside R&B superstar Ashanti, Ava Grace (Monarch), and more. The movie was produced by Sony Pictures Entertainment and released on Netflix.

2 Tessa Brooks Is Also A Philanthropist

As a philanthropist, Tessa works with the charity brand Karmagawa, and even opened her own school in Bali for children in poverty. Karmagawa is also linked to save the reef, a cause which Brooks is passionate about. She has a soft spot for kids and is passionate about advocating for those in unsafe homes, and works with causes that can help with getting them therapy or having a safe space.

In a 2019 interview with INLOVE Magazine, Brooks spoke about her love for dance and a need for her to share her passion with those who are less fortunate. “Dancing is such a beautiful art form because you can tell a story or express yourself in a way that words can’t… A thing I really want to make happen is teaching dance to kids who, due to circumstances beyond their control, can’t afford to take a dance class,” she shared.

1 How Tessa Brooks Spends Her Downtime

When she’s not trying to save the world, creating top-notch content, attending fashion shows or traveling around the world with her brand partners, Tessa Brooks enjoys watching films, going to beaches or museums, learning guitar, and delving into all things art. The creator enjoys exploring new ways to move that also sparks joy including hiking, Pilates, and boxing.
