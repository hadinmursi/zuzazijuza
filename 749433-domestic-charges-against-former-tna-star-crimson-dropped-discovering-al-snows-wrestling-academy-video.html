Domestic Charges Against Former TNA Star Crimson Dropped, Discovering Al Snows Wrestling Academy

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Crimson 

PWInsider.com is reporting all charges against former TNA Impact Wrestling star Crimson (real name Anthony Gregory Mayweather) have been dropped in relation to a past domestic assault charge back in April of this year. 

The attorney for Mayweather issued a statement to notify the charges have been dropped, and all records of the charges have been expunged from his record.

Mayweather was arrested and charged with domestic assault on April 3rd 2016 after a phone call notifying authorities of a man headbutting his wife in front of a hotel in Clarksville, Tennessee. At the time of incident, Mayweather was arrested and charged for the domestic assault, and his wife was also arrested and charged at the time for public intoxication.
