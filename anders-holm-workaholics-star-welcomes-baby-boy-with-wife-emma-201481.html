Anders Holm, Workaholics Star, Welcomes Baby Boy With Wife Emma

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Another job for this workaholic — dad duty! Anders Holm is a first-time father, Us Weekly can confirm. The Workaholics actor and his wife Emma Nesper welcomed a baby boy on Dec. 19, his rep tells Us exclusively.

Holm, 32, and his college sweetheart tied the knot in 2011, and have kept their relationship private for the most part. But on Monday, Jan. 6, the actor couldn't help but share a glimpse of his brand-new little bundle of joy to his Instagram followers.

In a video, Holm showed his adorable little tot crying while wearing brown and grey pajamas. Although the newborn is upset, his famous dad makes a joke about it by editing a loud voiceover into the clip.

Holm is best-known for starring in Comedy Central's Workaholics with fellow co-creators Adam DeVine and Blake Anderson. The three previously formed the sketch comedy group Mail Order Comedy in 2006. He also recently guest starred on the FOX comedy The Mindy Project as Mindy Kaling's love interest Casey.
