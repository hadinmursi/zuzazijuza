Taylor Swift Finds Sheep Rude, Unreasonable

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Rest easy, Olivia Benson and Meredith Grey. Taylor Swift won’t be replacing you as pets any time soon.

The singer made it clear that her loyalties lie with her cats yesterday after she apparently stopped by a farm of some kind and spent some very unpleasant time with a herd of sheep.

In one of our favorite Instagram videos ever, Swift quickly grows irritated with the animals in her presence because they continually mistake her hand "gestures" for "food."

And it’s just weird for Taylor. It’s just really weird and sort of annoying.
