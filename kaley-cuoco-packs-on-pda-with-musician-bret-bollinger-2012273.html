Kaley Cuoco Packs on PDA with Musician Bret Bollinger

Publish date: 2024-08-30

A formerly engaged Kaley Cuoco has been back on the singles market for less than a month, but it appears she’s already smooching someone new!

At the Lakers vs. Trailblazers basketball game in Los Angeles Friday, the Big Bang Theory actress was all smiles as she sat courtside with her beau, Pepper singer/bassist Bret Bollinger. (E! News first identified her new main squeeze Tuesday.)

Sporting a fedora, red leather leggings and a black sweater, the blonde star made it known she had nothing to hide as she packed on the PDA with her new man, who had his arm wrapped around his special lady. They even snapped a snuggly pic together with an iPhone.
