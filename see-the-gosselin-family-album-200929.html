Jon, Kate Gosselins Family Album With 8 Kids Over the Years: Pics

Publish date: 2024-09-07

It’s safe to say that life has changed a lot for Jon Gosselin and Kate Gosselin since their family landed their own reality show, Jon & Kate Plus 8, in 2007.

The couple, who wed in June 1999, documented their lives with twins Mady and Cara and sextuplets Collin, Hannah, Leah, Alexis, Aaden and Joel until the TLC show came to an end in in 2017. Amid the hit’s decade-long run, Jon and Kate called it quits after 10 years of marriage.

“Parents of multiples have triple the divorce rate,” the former labor and delivery nurse said in a 2009 confessional amid Jon’s cheating scandal. “I was thinking we were going to beat that. I don’t know if I can say that anymore.”

After the title was changed to Kate Plus 8, she went on to participate in more reality shows, competing on Dancing With the Stars with pro Tony Dovolani in 2010 and starring in Kate Plus Date nine years later. As for Jon, the Pennsylvania native has worked as a DJ, a waiter and an I.T. support specialist over the years.

The former couple’s kids no longer all live under one roof. In fact, Jon was awarded sole custody of Collin in December 2018 after the teenager attended a program for children with special needs. Hannah also resides with her dad. The rest of the sextuplets live with Kate. Mady and Cara, for their part, began dorming in their freshman years of college in 2020 at Syracuse University and Fordham University, respectively.

Jon has spoken candidly about his relationship with Mady, Cara, Leah, Alexia, Aaden and Joel, saying in 2018 that the last time he saw all of his children together was “not [a] good” experience.
