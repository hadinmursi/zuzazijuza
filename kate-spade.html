Kate Spade Biography TimeLine

Kate Spade was an American fashion designer and businesswoman. She was born Katherine Noel Brosnahan on December 24, 1962, in Kansas City, Missouri, and passed away on June 5, 2018, in New York City. Spade is best known for founding the fashion brand “Kate Spade New York” and for her eponymous line of handbags. Here is a timeline of her life and career.

1962: Born in Kansas City, Missouri, as Katherine Noel Brosnahan.

1986: She began her career in the fashion industry as an accessories editor at Mademoiselle magazine.

1993: Co-founded the fashion brand “Kate Spade New York” with her husband, Andy Spade.

1996: Launched her eponymous line of handbags.

1999: Won the CFDA Award for Accessories Designer of the Year.

2007: Sold her brand to the Neiman Marcus Group.

2017: Launched a new fashion brand called “Frances Valentine”.

2018: Passed away in New York City at the age of 55.

Kate Spade was a pioneering fashion designer and businesswoman who revolutionized the handbag industry. Her eponymous line of handbags was loved by women worldwide for its unique style and design. Despite her untimely passing, her impact on the fashion industry continues to be felt, and her brand remains an iconic and beloved symbol of American fashion.
