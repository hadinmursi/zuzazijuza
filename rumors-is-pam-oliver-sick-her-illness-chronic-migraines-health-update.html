Rumors: Is Pam Oliver Sick Her Illness Chronic Migraines &amp; Health Update

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Fans are understandably worried about Fox reporter Pam Oliver, who did not sound like herself on the sidelines of the early 2021 game.

Twitter was abuzz with speculation about the health of this Pam Oliver, whose speech at the Packers-Rams game sounded slightly garbled and slurred.

Speculations included her being intoxicated, having an aneurism or stroke, and freezing due to the weather, as well as being hit by a severe migraine attack. Here’s everything you need to know about American sportscaster’s illness.

Is Pam Oliver Sick? Her Illness Explored

Pam Oliver, 59, has had her fair share of health issues throughout her career. She told Yahoo Sports that she had fibroids “every two years, like clockwork” when she was in her twenties.

Debilitating migraines that cause vomiting, nausea, and excruciating pain have also plagued Oliver for years, sometimes in the middle of a game. They’ve gotten so bad that she hides out in stadium tunnels to avoid the noise, lights, and pyrotechnics.

Oliver has taken a proactive approach to migraine treatment, learning progressive muscle relaxation to relieve tension.

Every month, she also self-administers two shots of medication. “You just jam this thing in your thigh,” she explained as she demonstrated her shots.

It didn’t help when she was hit in the face with a ball during pregame warmups for the New York Giants-Indianapolis Colts preseason game in 2013 and suffered a concussion. She admitted to taking three to six Excedrin Migraine pills at a time to deal with the pain.

According to her own estimates, Pam has missed only four games in her 25-plus years with Fox.

Pam Oliver Chronic Migraines & Health Update

Previously, Pam Oliver encountered a health problem during an interview with Aaron Rodgers and Aaron Jones. Suddenly, she did not sound like herself.

Oliver remained silent about the incident. People made numerous comments about her health or illness. They even speculated that she was intoxicated.

Some speculated that she might be feeling cold due to the cold weather at Lambeau Field or that she might be extremely ill. Other groups were bullied as a result of her speech.

Sportscasting stated that she is a migraine sufferer who injects medication into herself when necessary. They speculated that her medication caused her slurring.

What Happened To Pam Oliver?

Pam Oliver was covering a playoff game between the Green Bay Packers and the Los Angeles Rams at Lambeau Field in the cold weather when the incident occurred.

In the third quarter, she began her report by providing an update on the Packers. Concerns were raised by viewers during her reporting when there were noticeable slurs and sluggishness in her speech.

While describing the Packers’ strategy to capitalize on Aaron Donald’s absence on the field, she had difficulty pronouncing words like “absences.”

During her postgame interviews with Aaron Rodgers and Aaron Jones, she was also seen struggling with these issues. The overall tone of her report was garbled and dissimilar to how she usually sounds.
