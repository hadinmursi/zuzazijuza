Who Is Mack Lovat And What Happened Between Him And Farrah Abraham?

Publish date: 2024-08-27

MTV viewers got acquainted with Farrah Abraham when she made her reality TV debut on 16 and Pregnant in 2009. Abraham would later join the Teen Mom cast, gaining popularity for her volatile relationships with fellow Teen Mom stars and her short-lived career in adult entertainment.

Despite leaving the Teen Mom franchise in 2017, Farah continues to make headlines. Most recently, the 31-year-old shocked the Teen Mom universe when she was arrested following an altercation with a security guard. The Teen Mom alum recently astounded fans yet again when she started and ended a relationship in less than 24 hours. Here’s everything we know about Farrah’s momentary boyfriend and their short-lived relationship.

8 What Does Mack Lovat Do For A Living?

Farrah Abraham’s most recent ex also works in the entertainment industry. While confirming their relationship to TMZ Farrah disclosed that her new beau was a guitarist with the hip-hop group Minus Gravity.

According to Farrah, Lovat is currently signed to the record label Capitol Records. Minus Gravity’s most popular song, Working For Free, was released in 2020 and has since garnered close to 400,000 views on YouTube.

7 How Mack Lovat And Farrah Abraham Met On Social Media

Mack Lovat and Farrah Abraham have known each other since 2020. The guitarist allegedly slid into Abraham’s Instagram DMs amid the COVID-19 pandemic and asked her out. The pair subsequently went on a date in the park and attended a basketball game together.

In a recent interview with TheAshley’s Reality Roundup, Mack Lovat confessed, “I asked her if she wanted to hang again but, barring a random DM convo we had about the Grammy’s a year or so ago, we didn’t speak until last week.”

6 When Did Mack Lovat And Farrah Abraham Start Dating?

Mack Lovat and Farrah Abraham reconnected earlier this week. The duo stepped out on a date to a popular sushi restaurant, flaunting PDA in full view of a horde of paparazzi.

During her run-in with TMZ, Farrah confessed that although things were not official yet, she and Lovat had taken a trip to Hawaii together on the Teen Mom OG’s birthday.

5 Why Did Mack Lovat And Farrah Abraham Break Up?

Despite starting on an incredibly high note, Farrah Abraham’s new relationship seemingly unraveled quite rapidly. Barely hours after going public with the relationship, Farrah Abraham announced that she decided to call it quits with Lovat.

Farrah confirmed the break-up to TMZ saying, "Sometimes people turn into monsters with press, happy I found out now." The Teen Mom alum also disclosed to E! News that she had “decided not to date Mack” because “he could not handle public attention well."

4 Mack Lovat Denies Dating Farrah Abraham​​​​​​​

Despite going on what turned out to be a PDA-packed sushi date, Mack Lovat was shocked by the news of his relationship with Farrah Abraham.

Shortly after Farrah’s announcement, the guitarist had an interview with The Ashley’s Reality Roundup where he denied ever dating Farrah claiming, “It was surprising to wake up and discover I had acquired a girlfriend overnight because I certainly never agreed to that.. with anyone.”

3 Mack Lovat Was Unaware Of Farrah Abraham’s Celebrity Status​

Despite knowing each other for two years and going on a romantic getaway to Hawaii, Mack Lovat insists that he had no clue that Farrah Abraham was a celebrity. The guitarist told The Ashley’s Reality Roundup, “To be fully transparent I wasn’t even aware of her fame when we initially met.”

Mack Lovat also denied sliding into Farrah’s Instagram DMs claiming, “I met Farrah on Bumble in 2020, and we took her pup Billionaire to a dog park. I most definitely did not ‘slide in her DMs.'"

2 Farrah Abraham Blocked Mack Lovat​​​​​​​

After being bombarded with rumors of their relationship, Mack Lovat reached out to Farrah Abraham for clarification over what, to him, was a shocking development.

In his interview with The Ashley’s Reality Roundup Lovat disclosed that he “sent Farrah a text to clarify this [her claims about their relationship], and to also let her know that I was under the impression something like that was a mutual decision… she then proceeded to serve me some words and block me.”

1 Farrah Abraham’s Insists She Dated Mack Lovat​​​​​

Shortly after Mack publicly denied their relationship, Farrah Abraham took to Instagram, posting a screenshot of what was, allegedly, a private text message exchange between herself and Lovat as proof of their romance.

The Teen Mom OG accompanied the screenshot with the words, “Don’t give anyone a chance anymore, don’t let people come out in public, unless they are public, & globally know, or I’ll have someone telling me we're dating but to the world, they don’t know me.”
