Selena Gomez Sizzles in High-Cut One-Piece Swimsuit: Beach Pics

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Miami heat! Selena Gomez soaked up the sun on the beach in Miami on Friday, Sept. 18, in a sizzling black one-piece swimsuit.

The “Same Old Love” singer, 23, rocked the skimpy garment, only $48 at American Apparel, all the better to reveal a new tattoo of the om symbol high up on her thigh.

Gomez’s swimwear comes on the heels of the high-cut one-piece bathing suit’s apparent rise once again, as it’s been spotted on multiple celebs, including Kendall Jenner, Kylie Jenner, Rihanna and Beyonce. These pieces bring to mind the same Baywatch-worthy hip bone-baring suits popular in the 1990s.
