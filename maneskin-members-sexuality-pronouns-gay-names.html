Måneskin is an Italian glam rock band formed in Rome in 2016. The band is composed of vocalist Damiano David, bassist Victoria De Angelis, guitarist Thomas Raggi, and drummer Ethan Torchio.

The Italian rock band rose to prominence after competing on the eleventh season of X-Factor Italia.

In 2021, they crowned the winners of both the Sanremo Music Festival and the Eurovision Song Contest.
