Hulu Announces Streaming Rights Deal With WWE Is Expiring

Publish date: 2024-08-28

It surely appears that weekly WWE programming on Hulu might be coming to an end.

Earlier today, it was announced that Friday Night SmackDown would be returning to the USA Network in October 2024. The current status of WWE RAW and NXT remains unknown.

But there’s a chance despite where WWE programs land in 2024, they won’t be available to watch on-demand with Hulu.

Hulu responded to a member of the WWE Universe on social media about the company’s programming on the platform. In doing so, they revealed their current streaming rights deal is set to expire, tweeting out:

“Hulu here! Our streaming rights to WWE content are currently set to expire, but we’ll certainly share your interest in seeing them stay in our library with our team. For now, we suggest adding the shows in your My Stuff to be notified of any updates!” Hulu said in a post.

The company has yet to comment in regards to its current status with Hulu.
