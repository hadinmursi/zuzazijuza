Who Is Chris Snow? Everything To Know About The Assistant General Manager Of The Calgary Flames

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Chris Snow does not have a profile on Wikipedia. In 2019, Chris was diagnosed with ALS, and since 2019 he has been fighting his battle with it. 

Chris Snow is Assistant General Manager Of The Calgary Flames.

Chris Snow’s Wikipedia

Wikipedia has not listed Chris Snow on their website.

Chris was a journalist who covered the Red Sox for The Boston Globe.

In 2011, Chris was hired as the director of hockey analysis for the Flames.

Chris is 38 years old.

Chris Snow’s Diagnosis Of ALS

In June 2019, Chris Snow was diagnosed with ALS. He was given 6-18 months to live.

Als is also known as Lou Gehrig’s disease. It causes the muscles in a person’s body to break down by gradually paralyzing.

Who Is Chris Snow Married To?

Chris is married to Kelsie Snow. 

Kelsie is a journalist and freelance writer. She has covered Major League Baseball and other professional sports for the Boston Globe, Minneapolis Star Tribune, and Los Angeles Times.

Chris was 23, and she was 21 at the time they met.

Together they have been blessed with two children.

He has a son and a daughter: Cohen and Willa.
