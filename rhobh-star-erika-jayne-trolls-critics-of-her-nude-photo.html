“You sound ashamed of your body. I’m not,” Janye clarified.

“What does your hubby think about this pic?” one asked.

“He thinks it [sic] great,” Jayne answered, adding, “Bet yours does too … don’t bother responding. Thanks.”

“YOUR HUSBAND LIKE THIS PICTURE I AM SURE HE IS PROUD OF YOU LOLLLLLLL UNBELIEVABLE WOW NO RESPECT,” another post read.
