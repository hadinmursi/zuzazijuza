Bachelor Ben Flajnik Lies About Multiple Affairs on National TV

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Ben Flajnik's father didn't raise him to be a liar — or did he?

During Monday's After the Final Rose special, the 29-year-old California winemaker denied cheating on fiancee Courtney Robertson, 28 — despite photographic evidence to the contrary.

"On my father's grave, I haven't kissed another woman. I haven't hooked up with another woman. I haven't cheated on Courtney. None of that," he told host Chris Harrison, 40. "Those are friends from San Francisco. Those are past photos."

Unfortunately for Flajnik, the evidence is undeniable. As first seen in Us Weekly, The Bachelor star cheated on his bride-to-be with three different women in one weekend in mid-February.

Flajnik adamantly denied groping and making out with one particular woman by a waterfront park near the Golden Gate Bridge — but Us has the photographic proof, taken just before midnight February 18.

Said a witness: "Ben did not seem to care who saw him, considering his status on a prime-time reality show about finding true love."
