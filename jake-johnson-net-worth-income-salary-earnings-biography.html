Jake Mark Johnson (born May 28, 1978) is an American actor and comedian who is best known for his role as Nick Miller in the Fox comedy series New Girl, opposite Zooey Deschanel, for which he has received numerous nominations, including a Teen Choice Award nomination. Johnson has also appeared in films such as Paper Heart (2009) and Safety Not Guaranteed (2012), as well as Get Him to the Greek (2009) and 21 Jump Street (2012). The 2014 comedy Let’s Be Cops marked his feature film debut, and he also appeared in the film Drinking Buddies, in which he co-starred with Damon Wayans, Jr., who he met while working on New Girl.
