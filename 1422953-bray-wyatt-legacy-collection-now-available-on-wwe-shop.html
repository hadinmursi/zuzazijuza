Bray Wyatt Legacy Collection Now Available On WWE Shop

Publish date: 2024-08-28

WWE continues to highlight the legacy of Bray Wyatt.

Windham Rotunda, also known as Bray Wyatt in WWE, passed away suddenly on August 24, and the wrestling world mourned his loss.

Now, WWE has launched the Bray Wyatt Legacy Collection on WWE Shop, and it includes new Legacy Collection t-shirts. The lineup also includes past Wyatt t-shirts, The Fiend’s mask, Uncle Howdy’s mask, and commemorative plaques.

Wyatt was one of WWE’s top stars throughout his time with the company. He won the WWE Championship, and he also captured the Universal Championship twice.
