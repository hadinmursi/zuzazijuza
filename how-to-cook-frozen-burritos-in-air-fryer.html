Can you put frozen burritos in an air fryer?

Preheat your Air Fryer to 400F / 200C. Place frozen burritos into the air fryer basket. Air fry at 12-15 minutes from frozen, making sure to flip a couple of times during the cooking process.

How long do you cook El Monterey burritos in air fryer?

Set your air fryer to 350 degrees F for 15 minutes total. You want to flip your El Monterey burritos every 5 minutes so they end up with sealed sides facing up again.

  • Place the burrito on a cookie sheet and slide it onto the top rack of your oven.

    How do you make frozen burritos not soggy?

    Preventing soggy burritos
  • Make sure that the filling is room temperature before rolling the burritos.
  • Roll them tightly to keep out moisture.
  • Wrap them in plastic wrap and then a sheet of aluminum foil.
  • Reheat them in the oven or in a toaster oven in order to get a crisper tortilla.
