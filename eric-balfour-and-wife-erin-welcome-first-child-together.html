Eric Balfour and Wife Erin Welcome First Child Together

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Baby makes three! Actor Eric Balfour has welcomed his first child with wife Erin, Us Weekly can confirm.

The 24 alum’s longtime love reportedly gave birth to a baby boy, whose name has yet to be revealed, on Thursday, August 2, in Santa Monica, California. The little one weighed in at 7 lbs, 6 oz.

“This is the most amazing day of our lives and couldn’t be happier to welcome this perfect being into the world,” the Six Feet Under actor, 41, told Us.

Balfour announced his wife’s pregnancy in May. “We’re pretty lucky to have each other … I think the little baby boy growing in Erin’s belly is gonna be super stoked to hang out with us,” he captioned a cute shot of the couple with their dog, Coconut.
