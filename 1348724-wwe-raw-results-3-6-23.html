WWE RawResults(3/6/23)

Publish date: 2024-09-03

 

Check out last week’s coverage: WWE SmackDown Results (3/3/23)

Paul Heyman arrived at the arena with solo Sikoa and Jimmy Uso earlier today. Heyman reminds Jimmy that Roman Reigns wanted Sami Zayn handled last week. They failed. Tonight, Sikoa will solve the Kevin Owens problem, and Jimmy will solve the Sami Zayn problem tonight, forever.

Kevin Owens vs. Solo Sikoa

Owens attacks Sikoa. The fight spills out of the ring. Sikoa tosses Owens back in the ring, and the bell rings. Owens tackles Sikoa. Sikoa runs over Owens and takes control. Sikoa works over Owens. Owens fires up and lands a flurry of offense. Jimmy runs down to ringside as Owens mounts the top rope. In the confusion, Sikoa sends Owens careening out of the ring. Sikoa hip attacks Owens into the barricade. After the break, Owens catches Sikoa with three superkicks in a row. Owens lands a swanton. Jimmy hits the ring and attacks Owens. The referee throws the match out.

Winner- Kevin Owens

After the match, Jimmy and Sikoa beat down Owens. Jimmy lays Owens on the commentary desk. Sikoa sets up a splash. Sami Zayn hops the barricade and attacks Sikoa and Jimmy. Zayn chases the Bloodline off with a steel chair. Owens rolls back into the ring. Zayn offers Owens a hand. Owens rolls out of the ring and leaves.

Backstage, Bobby Lashley demands Bray Wyatt face him like a man.

Bianca Belair vs. Carmella w/Chelsea Green

Carmella takes an early advantage. After the break, Belair fires up. Green gets in the ring to distract the referee while Belair attempts a pin. Low superkick by Carmella. Belair kicks out. Belair traps Green in the corner and lays in the ten punches. Green gets in the ring and is quickly tossed over the top by Belair. Belair follows Green out of the ring and launches her into the crowd. Belair drops Carmella with the KOD for the win.

Winner- Bianca Belair

After the match, Green and Carmella work over Belair. Asuka’s music hits, and she stomps to the ring. Carmella has seen this movie before, so she ducks when Asuka hits the ring and spits the mist. Green is covered in blue mist. Belair and Asuka stare each other down.

Miz TV

The Miz welcomes his guest, Logan Paul. Miz tries to shake Paul’s hand, but Paul ignores him. Rollins’ music hits immediately, and he joins them in the ring. The crowd won’t stop singing Rollins’ music. Paul tells everyone to shut up. Rollins quips that though they have had their run-ins. they have never been formally introduced. Rollins runs down his many titles. Paul says he is the guy who dumped Rollins at the Royal Rumble and stopped his head in at Elimination Chamber.

He’s flat-out better than Rollins is at what Rollins does. If Paul were Rollins, he’d hate Paul too. Miz says Paul has some good points. Rollins actually agrees, but Paul is wrong about why we don’t like him. Paul is the scum of the earth, a piece of crap, a fraud, and more. We don’t want Paul here. Rollins yells the time for talk is over, and it’s time to fight. Miz says it’s his job to make sure they don’t fight. Paul adds that he doesn’t need Miz to speak for him. If he wants to fight Rollins in Boston, he will. But he’s not going to. He doesn’t fight for free, and he isn’t fighting in Boston.

Paul says he would consider it if it were in a place with a bit more star power. Miz says he’s the host of WrestleMania, so he can make Paul vs. Rollins happen at WrestleMania. Rollins asks Miz if he could really do that. Miz says yes. He just has to go in the back and speak to someone. Rollins says, bet, do it, and tossed Miz over the top rope. Rollins double legs, Paul. Miz grabs Rollins from behind. Superkick by Rollins. Paul obliterates Rollins with a right hand that knocks him out cold.
