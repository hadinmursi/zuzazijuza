How do I turn off Alexa profanity filter?

Turn Explicit Filtering On or Off in the Alexa App
  • Open the Alexa app .
  • Open More and select Settings.
  • Select Music & Podcasts, and then Explicit Language Filter.
  • Switch the feature on or off.

    Can I change Alexa’s name?

    Changing the device name

    In the Android or iOS apps, click the hamburger button in the upper left corner to expand the side menu. In the side menu, select Settings. Click on one of the devices under Alexa Devices to open the settings specific to that device. Type in a new name and click Save Changes.
