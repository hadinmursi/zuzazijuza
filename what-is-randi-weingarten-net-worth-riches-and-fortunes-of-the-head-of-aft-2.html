Randi Weingarten Bio

Randi Weingarten was born and reared in Rockland County, New York. Her father was an electrical engineer and her mother was an elementary school teacher.

She accompanied her mother on the picket line of a teachers union strike while she was in eleventh grade.

In terms of her schooling, Weingarten graduated from Cornell University with a bachelor’s degree in labor relations in 1980.

Following in the year 1983, she earned a law degree from Yeshiva University’s Cardozo School of Law.

In the year 2008, she was elected president of the national American Federation of teachers.
