Britney Spears Causes Mass Speculation After Expressing Interest In Brittany Murphy's Death

Publish date: 2024-08-30

In 2009, the world mourned the tragic passing of Clueless star Brittany Murphy. She was only 32. The official coroner's verdict cited her death as pneumonia, exacerbated by anemia and addiction to prescription pills. But many fans of the late actress have always speculated that foul play was involved.

Britney Spears Once Lived In The Same House Brittany Murphy Died In

In a since deleted post, pop singer Britney Spears voiced her suspicion over Brittany Murphy. The Girl, Interrupted star is said to have bought her Hollywood Hills home from Spears in the late 2000s when she was still dating Justin Timberlake.

Re-posting a cover of People magazine feature regarding Murphy's death from October last year, the "Baby...One More Time" singer captioned it: "I saw this online today …. is anybody else curious ??? She died at 32 … HMMMMMMMMMMM… JUST SAYING !!!! Psss I know it was a while ago but come on …they are STILL INVESTIGATING !!!!"

Brittany Murphy's Late Husband Has Been Described As 'Disturbed'

The People spread last year coincided with the docu-series What Happened, Brittany Murphy? It detailed Murphy's last few months with husband Simon Monjack - who also died age 40 under the same extraordinary circumstances. His death came just five months after his wife's. The director of the two part series, Cynthia Hill, described Monjack "as a disturbed individual who was used to conning people."

Brittany Murphy Fans Took To Social Media To Comment On Her Death

After Britney Spears posted about the mysterious death of Brittany Murphy, fans took to Instagram to speculate.

"Brittany Murphy was so beautiful and talented but something about her was definitely...tragic..not sure what other word to use," one comment read online.

"I hope if someone is responsible for Brittney Murphy's death it will come out. I am highly suspicious of her mother," a second added.

"I always thought it was suspicious. The fact that she died and then he also died shortly afterwards. The toxic mould in the house was supposedly the cause, but this is Hollywood we're talking about," a third commented.
